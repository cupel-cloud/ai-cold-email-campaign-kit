# 🎯 Company Scoring & Tiering Criteria — TiltIt (Video Editing Agencies)

> TiltIt: Browser-based tool that turns flat screen recordings into cinematic 3D animated videos. $79 one-time Pro. Target: creators, SaaS demos, tutorials, pitch decks, social clips.

## Scoring Framework (0-100)

| Factor | Max Points | Description |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| Industry Fit | 25 | Video editing, post-production, content creation agencies |
| Company Size | 20 | Sweet spot 10-100 employees |
| Service Offering | 20 | SaaS demos, product videos, social content, tutorials |

## Factor Scoring Details

### Industry Fit (0-25 pts)
| Match Level | Points | Examples |
|-------------|--------|---------|
| Core match | 25 | Video editing agency, post-production house, content creation studio |
| Strong adjacency | 20 | Marketing agency with video dept, SaaS demo production |
| Moderate fit | 12 | General creative agency, freelance video editors |
| Weak fit | 5 | Web design agency, photography studio |
| No fit | 0 | Film/TV production, non-media industry |

### Company Size (0-20 pts)
| Employees | Points | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| 1-5 | 5 | Too small — likely solo editors, limited budget |
| 6-15 | 12 | Small team, could benefit but limited scale |
| 16-50 | 20 | **Sweet spot** — enough editors to see ROI, fast adoption |
| 51-100 | 18 | Great — multiple teams, strong multiplier effect |
| 101-200 | 12 | Good but may have established workflows |
| 200+ | 6 | Slow to adopt new tools, procurement hoops |

### Service Offering (0-20 pts)
| Service Type | Points | Why |
|-------------|--------|-----|
| SaaS product demos & walkthroughs | 20 | **Perfect fit** — screen recordings are their core input |
| Tutorial & e-learning videos | 18 | Heavy screen recording usage |
| Social media content & short-form | 16 | Need fast, cinematic output |
| Product marketing videos | 14 | Often includes screen recordings |
| Corporate video & brand content | 10 | Some screen recording, mixed |
| Film/TV / documentary | 3 | Wrong market — no screen recordings |
| Wedding / event video | 2 | Not relevant |

### Tech Stack (0-15 pts)
| Signal | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Uses screen recording tools (Loom, ScreenFlow, Camtasia, ScreenPal) | 15 |
| Uses After Effects or motion graphics tools | 12 |
| Uses Descript or AI video editing tools | 10 |
| Uses Adobe Premiere or DaVinci Resolve | 8 |
| Uses only basic editing (CapCut, iMovie) | 5 |
| No visible tech stack | 2 |

### Revenue (0-10 pts)
| Revenue | Points | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| $2M-$5M | 10 | Has budget, sees ROI at scale |
| $500K-$2M | 8 | Growing, smart about tool investments |
| $200K-$500K | 5 | Budget-conscious, $79 one-time is attractive |
| <$200K | 3 | Tight budget but one-time pricing helps |
| $5M+ | 6 | Has budget but may prefer enterprise tools |

### Growth Signals (0-10 pts)
| Signal | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Actively hiring editors or motion designers | 10 |
| Expanding service offerings (adding demos/tutorials) | 8 |
| New client wins or case studies posted | 6 |
| Growing social presence / content output | 4 |
| Stable / no visible growth | 1 |

## Tiering Thresholds

| Tier | Score | Strategy |
|------|-------|----------|
| 🥇 **Tier 1** | 75-100 | Highly personalized outreach, direct value prop or case study framework |
| 🥈 **Tier 2** | 50-74 | Semi-personalized, PAS framework, emphasize $79 one-time pricing |
| 🥉 **Tier 3** | 25-49 | Template-based, social proof framework, light touch |
| ❌ **Disqualified** | <25 | Do not contact |

---

## Disqualification Criteria (Instant DQ)

- ❌ Film/TV production only (no screen recording use case)
- ❌ Already using TiltIt
- ❌ No video editing services (pure design, dev, etc.)
- ❌ Company shutting down / acquired
- ❌ No discoverable email contacts
- ❌ On DNC/blacklist
